Jollof Rice

>

This delicious recipe combines fresh ingredients with simple cooking techniques to create a meal that is both flavorful and easy to prepare. The balance of spices and textures makes it a favorite for all occasions.

Perfect for weeknight dinners or special gatherings, this dish is sure to impress. Follow the instructions carefully, and enjoy the delightful results!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the milk and vanilla extract to the egg mixture and mix well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased baking dish.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the dish to cool before serving. Enjoy!
